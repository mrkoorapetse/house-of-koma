About

House of Koma is a space where sophistication meets sound, a curated house music experience designed for discerning ears and cultured souls an immersive House music event designed to celebrate the deep, rhythmic sounds of African-electronic culture, mainly house music and its sub-genres. This is an event for those who appreciate quality over hype, community over chaos, and timeless house music that moves the mind, body and soul This will be the first edition, first of its kind in kweneng district and we aim to grow into a recurring, community-driven experience, shining a spotlight on emerging talent, connecting like-minded music lovers and amplifying the House music scene nationwide “This is House music elevated.”

Our Vision

To create Botswana’s most inspiring house music experience, a vibrant gathering that unites people through rhythm, celebrates our cultural values, and redefines nightlife as clean, safe, and uplifting. We aspire to build a tradition where the energy of world-class house music blends seamlessly with the spirit of Botho, leaving a lasting mark on our community and the entertainment scene.

Our Mission

To host a premier house music event that delivers an exceptional, safe, and inclusive nightlife experience. Through top-class performances, impeccable organisation, and a commitment to cleanliness and respect, we aim to bring people together, showcase the richness of our culture, and set a new benchmark for responsible entertainment in Botswana.

What to Expect

  • Top DJs & live performers
  • Immersive lighting and visuals
  • Safe, curated event spaces
